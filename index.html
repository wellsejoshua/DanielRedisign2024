Who Is Daniel Fears?

From my beginnings in the church to singing and producing soulful music from my heart, I have always had a passion for composition. This love led me to become classically trained in trombone fusing classical with soul.

Through support from my fans in a crowd funding campaign, we were able to raise money to put my singles to strings while paying local artist a fare wage.

I have been able to form friendships and partnerships whit some of my favorite people. I invite you to continue this journey with me and help create something special.
